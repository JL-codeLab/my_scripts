Matthew 6:33
"But seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be given to you as well."

1. But, Coordinating Adversative Conjunction.
2. seek, to actively attempt to find (tentativa ativa de procurar), acquire (to get), or achieve something (to successfully reach a goal after hard work or overcoming obstacles). "To seek" is the infinitive verb and "seek" is the order mode.
3. his, Possessive Adjective (pâzessiv)
4. ness, state/property/condition or quality of something
- righteousness
- brightness
- darkness
- greatness
- weakness
- hapiness
5. this - these
   that - those
6. will, have a function before the verbs and without them, it means the willing or desire of someone
